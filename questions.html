Supercell Data Scientist Pre-Test

You've been selected for Supercell Data Scientist pre-screening. Please solve the following tasks.

We'd prefer that you use R and SQLite3, but you may also use Python, Excel or other generally used toolkits you consider suitable for industry use.

The best ways to access a SQLite3 database is the RSQLite package in R or the sqlite3 command line interface.

Please return your solutions as a PDF file to datascience-prescreen@supercell.net and attach all your code and plots. Please send your plots in PDF or PNG format.

Task 1. How does the revenue trickle?

You've received a SQLite3 database tasks.sqlite as an attachment.

The database contains three tables: account, account_device and transactions. account contains user profiles, account_device their devices and transactions contains in-app purchases.

  • How much revenue was produced on 2013-02-01?
  • Are there any users who use both iPads and iPhones?
  • Which country produces the most revenue?
  • What is the iPad/iPhone split in Canada?
  • What proportion of lifetime revenue is generated on the player's first week in game?
